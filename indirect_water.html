Indirect Fired Water Heaters

Efficiency

Operating as a separate zone off your boiler, the sidearm tank will provide hot water at the same efficiency level as your boiler, which is typically much more efficient than a standard gas water heater. In addition, because the boiler typically has a large BTU capacity in order to heat your home, it can quickly re-heat the tank. Since there is no direct heat source, it operates in a low-stress environment for a very long service life.
