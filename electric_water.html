Electric Water Heaters

Advantages

Save Home Heat carries electric water heaters for the limited applications where a gas fired system is not feasible. Electric tanks are reliable, low maintenance, easy to install and eliminate safety concerns with gas combustion. If your home has a significant photovoltaic (PV) array, an electric tank has cost savings advantages as well. Electric water heaters take longer to heat up after the water has been used.
