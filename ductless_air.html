Ductless Air Conditioners

Ductless Air

A ductless air conditioner is a quiet, super-efficient system used to cool any part of your home or business. An attractive indoor unit is mounted on an interior wall and cool air is distributed quietly. A small, quiet condensing unit is located outside the house to generate the cooling. The system is controlled from an easy to use remote control, or a wall mounted thermostat can be added instead.
