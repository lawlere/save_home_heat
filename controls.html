Thermostats

We carry a wide range of thermostats, from models that do nothing but 'turn it up and down', to models that can control your humidifier, or be controlled while you're out of town, or even models with a remote you can carry from room to room so your comfort setting moves with you.

Contact us for a free quote.

Zone Systems

In new construction settings, forced air or hot water heat systems can be designed with multiple thermostats to allow for different temperature settings in different areas of the home. In existing homes, this arrangement is considerably more challenging, but occasionally still feasible. If this arrangement sounds interesting, please call to learn more.
