Air Filtration and Cleaning

Air Filters

Air filters are a fabric or mesh, usually ranging from 1-inch to 5-inches thick, that remove particles from the air coming back to your furnace. We have basic, low cost, 1-inch filters that need to be changed regularly, and thicker, more effective, higher cost filters that can often retain their effectiveness for 6-months to a year. One of our estimators or service technicians can suggest a product to meet your needs.
