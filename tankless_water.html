Tankless Water Heaters

Features

Continuous stream of hot water (typically up to 3 - 4 gallons per minute)

Very high efficiencies (Energy Factor of approximately .82 and above)

Sealed combustion operation (safe, quiet, efficient)

Saves space (much smaller than a storage tank)

Often qualifies for rebates

Reduced resource consumption and reduced carbon footprint

Contact us for a free quote.

Disadvantages

More complex system requiring annual maintenance.

Limited to 3-4 gallon per hour flow rate (may not supply multiple hot water uses at once).

More costly to install.

May be barriers to installation (potential exhaust pipe and gas line issues).

Contact us for a free quote.

Advantages

Tankless water heaters are expected to have a longer life span and are considerably more efficient than high efficiency tank-type water heaters. An excellent product to consider for the energy conscious customer.
