Furnaces

Efficiency

Gas Efficiency: We offer furnace with gas efficiencies as high as a remarkable 98%! Older standard furnaces range from 60%-80% efficient.

Electrical Efficiency: Furnace are available with super-efficient electronically commutated motors (ECM) or constant torque motors to drastically reduced the electrical operating cost.

We have a broad selection of furnaces to satisfy your efficiency goals.

Quiet

Sealed combustion furnaces have closed burning chambers and doors with gaskets to isolate operating noise inside the furnace.

Two-stage and modulating furnaces operate at reduced BTU and fan levels to minimize noise through the duct system.

Electronically commutated motors can operate at very low fan speeds when you just want to circulate air. Many older furnaces were oversized, making them louder than necessary.

Combine all these factors together and you may end up with a significantly quieter furnace!

Comfortable

Two-stage and modulating furnaces have longer running cycles to more evenly distribute heat throughout your home.

With an electronically commutated motor you can leave the fan on to quietly and efficiently clean the air in your home and produce more balanced temperatures.

If your home doesn't heat evenly, we can help!
