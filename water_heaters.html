Our Water Heaters

Standard Water Heaters

There have been some minor tweaks along the way to modernize the gas control and the ignition process, but this is still a basic, reliable tank of hot water standing ready for use when you need it.

Find out more.

Tankless Water Heaters

Tankless water heaters apply a large quantity of heat to instantly warm domestic water for immediate use. While you can create only so much hot water at once, it can flow for as long as needed.

Find out more.

Indirect Fired Water Heaters

Indirect fired water heaters, also known as sidearm tanks, are ideal if your home is heated by a boiler (hot water heat).

Find out more.

Electric Water Heaters

Save Home Heat carries electric water heaters for the limited applications where a gas fired system is not feasible. If you have hot water heat, we strongly recommend an indirect fired water heater for your domestic hot water!
