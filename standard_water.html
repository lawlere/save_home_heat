High Efficiency Options

Some tanks can achieve the .67 Energy Factor (EF rating) needed to qualify for local rebate programs. High efficiency models are more complex and cost considerably more to install. Economic payback is not a strong selling point. These tanks are ideally suited for those with strong energy conservation goals or the need to eliminate the 'drafting effect' of standard tanks.
